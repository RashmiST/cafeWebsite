About us

Our mission

Cafe Cafe opened in Nagpur, VA in 2014. Our mission at cafe Cafe is to offer our customers wholesome and delicious deli meats and cheeses, accompanied with excellent customer service.

Cafe market

The cafe cafe is proud to serve fresh, made-from-scratch food, featuring Boar's Head brand meats and cheeses. Boar's Head products have no fillers, artificial colors, flavors, or trans fats. They are also gluten free! Our menu has plenty to offer, with great things for any time of the day. Breakfast items, sandwiches, soups, paninis, and salads are right here waiting.
